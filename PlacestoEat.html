House of Small Wonder

Part Japanese eating house, part hipster café, House of Small Wonder is filled with plants and is one of the coolest eateries in Berlin. It features in many Berlin city guides and is a top destination for brunch. It is styled as a greenhouse ‒ a beautiful one ‒ and its food menu is equally as striking, with Japanese twists on American dishes.

Café Anna Blume

Named after a poem by German dada and surrealist artist Kurt Schwitters, Café Anna Blume is a gem in the heart of Berlin’s Prenzlauer neighbourhood. Part coffee shop, part flower shop, it is one of the most photogenic cafés in the capital. There are stunning flowers occupying in the space outside; inside

Jubel

Candy-coloured tiles and chic counters make Jubel a must-visit for those seeking Instagram material in Berlin. The café offers exceptional cakes and pastries which add to the prettiness and are renowned for their light and inventive qualities.

+FACTORY GIRL!

Berlin's Factory Girl was born in 2010, with a hip, stylish and cozy café culture that dynamically blends with the land of poets and thinkers. The atmosphere bursts with warmth, friendliness, coolness and charm. FACTORY GIRL is a gifted and brilliant gastronomic idea that draws from expertise shared from all over the world.

Benedict

Benedict a place where breakfast is favored all day long – morning, mid-day, afternoon, evening or night.Benedict is a fusion of a substantial portion of Tel-Avivian nonchalance, a dash of urban New York chic and a pinch of European bohemian charm, creating a tasty culinary journey around the globe.

CHIPPS

The Chipps offers vegetarian and vegan cuisine for various times of the day. Guests can make their start into the day with an awesome breakfast. For example: waffles with blueberry jam and cream, French Toast with vegan bacon or rustig egg dishes like an omelette or Eggs Benedict.
