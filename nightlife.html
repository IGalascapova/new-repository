Watergate

Watergate is a split-level, two-room club overlooking the River Spree in Berlin's Kreuzberg neighbourhood. Located on the river's edge, it offers a panoramic view of the Spree. Known for its LED lighting panel that spans, Watergate mostly plays host to house and techno parties.

Visit

Berghain

Located on the border of Kreuzberg and Friedrichshain, Berghain is widely considered one of the world's best nightclubs. It has three dance floors where most DJs play techno.

Visit

KitKatClub

The KitKatClub is a nightclub in Berlin, opened in March 1994 by Austrian pornographic film maker Simon Thaur and his life partner Kirsten Krüger.

Visit

Tresor

Legendary German techno club and label with releases from Blake Baxter, Jeff Mills, Juan Atkins, Joey Beltram, Robert Hood, Surgeon and many more.

Visit

Matrix

If you are sick of Berlin techno clubs and just want to have a good time and dance to some great tunes, then head to Matrix. Open every single night of the year, they have 7 dance floors.

Visit

Salon zur wilden Renate

The Wilde Renate will remind you of the beginnings of Berlin’s techno scene. Improvised and euphoric, alive instead of cool. The two floors feature minimal, electro and dubstep, concerts and performances.

Visit

Chalet

Located in Kreuzberg, Chalet offers a great line up on two different floors and a very charming garden. The best word to describe Chalet is style. It’s impossible not to notice the amazing decoration: vintage lamps and armchairs, bonfires in the backyard, wallpapers with antique patterns and many small rooms.

Visit

Sisyphos

Sisyphos is a place like no other. It’s a festival, it’s a club, it’s a beach, it’s a bar, it’s Alice’s Wonderland. You’ll feel like you’re in a world of joy, happiness and freedom.
